Descent

Poems born from loss, emptiness, surrender, and existence.

No. 01

Is Happiness Gone?

Is happiness gone?

Is Does Sorrow yet to comes it matter?

For what is emotion to whom who has lost the has will to live?

No. 02

Pity Those Eyes

Pity those Eyes,

for they have seen too much.

Pity that heart,

for it beats only in sorrow.

Pity that soul,

for it wonders out rest.

Even in the garden of flowers,

all he feels are the thorns.

No. 03

Whenever the Wind Takes Me

Whenever the wind takes me I shall go,

for I am just a mere sand particle in this beach

I have no morals, no virtues, no desires,

for all was given and none was taken,

all I am is a pawn for the greater good,

yet I stand with no purpose at all.

No. 04

The Concert Death Scene

Imagine. Like I just completed a concert.

On my way down behind the curtains.

Noone can see ne.

I get sudden heart pain.

My heart is failing.

I clutch my chest.

I don't want to call for help.

I just think.

Ig this is it god?

I take support of a wall.

Then get down on my knees.

All I can hear are the screams of the audiance.

Saying how great I was.

On the floor.

I get peak satisfaction.

With no sound.

No bang.

No outro.

Just like a wind has swept.

How an eagle kills a sparrow.

No sound.

I lay on the ground.

Unseen by none.

But my creator.

My guitar lays on my side.

Just like on the birth.

Wind blows.

Clouds emerges.

Rain starts to pour.

Thunders.

The animals look above for a minute.

The thunder rumbles.

Like it did when I came.

I shall go.

Unseen by none.

Known by many.

Praised by few.

Loved by none.

May I find salvation.

No. 28

I Am Him

It's not in Temple I find him;

It's in the darkest of sky night,

With the brightness of moon,

Wirh the loudest of thunder,

With heaviest of rain.

In the thunder I hear him,

In the sky I see him,

In the air I feel him,

As if he's right there in the sky,

Gretting me with open arms,

The chills running down my spine is his hug,

Cold yet inviting,

I don't want to go in warmth,

This IS my warmth,

Behind me is his shadow,

Yet he stands in front,

So huge can't even fathom his size or shape,

Yet so small he fits in my heart,

I am here,

I am with him,

He is me,

I am him.
