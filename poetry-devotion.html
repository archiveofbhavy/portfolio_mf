Devotion

Poems written in love, longing, attachment, and eternity.

No. 01

For Eternity

I shall wait for you,

For eternity if god asks,

Just so I could have that one blissful moment.

No. 02

Thorns and Roses

Show me your thorns my rose

and I'll show you the hands

willing to bleed for you.

No. 03

Blind With Purpose

I would rather stay blind in a world where you don't exist,

because witnessing your beauty

gave me the realisation

of why God gave me these eyes.

No. 04

Mad in Love

I would rather stay a mad man in love

than a sane without purpose.

No. 05

More Than Words

For the words aren't enough,

A book wouldn't suffice,

A poem wouldn't explain,

A story wouldn't tell,

A book wouldn't show,

What I feel towards you

is something even god himself

would be happy to feel.

No. 06

One Last Kiss

If I was on my death bed,

I would use my last bit of energy

just to roll over

and kiss you goodbye

for one last time.

No. 07

The Best Symphony

Even the worst musician

will play the best symphony

when his love is in the audience.

No. 08

A Wish Without End

If I had to make a wish for us together,

even a whole century wouldn't suffice

and I would still fight god

for another millennia

with my true love.

No. 09

Unbound by Time

If love could be bound by time,

the moon wouldn't still be rotating earth

after millions of years.

No. 10

Undying Love

There's two things I am best at.

The second is poetry.

You know the first?

My undying love for you.

No. 11

On the Bookshelf

Even if you leave,

Just keep an eye out

on the bookshelves.

You might find a book

dedicated to your love.

No. 12

My Radha

In the midst of Navratri,

every year till I die,

my eyes will only search

for a glimpse of you,

my Radha.

No. 13

Forever

My eyes will search you forever,

My heart will beat for you forever,

My mind will think of you forever,

My mouth will only speak about you.

I will always love you.

No. 14

Sunshine & Moonlight

You're the morning sunshine

to my midnight moonlight.

No. 15

Even After Death

Even if you decide to move on,

I will stay back,

cherishing the memories with you.

I genuinely love you.

I will always love you.

Even after my death,

my soul will always look for yours.

No. 16

Drowning in Honey

I hate how much I love you,

however sweet it may be,

I find myself drowning in honey.

No. 17

Close Enough

To stay close to you,

I want every part of me

to be touching you.

No. 18

In You

In your eyes,

I see my best self.

In your heart,

I hear the beats that match mine.

In your mind,

I see the image

that compares me to god.

In your soul,

I see the happiness

my soul felt

when I gazed upon you

the first time.

No. 19

You Make Me

You don't complete me.

You make me something

I could never see.

You make me someone

even god would appreciate.

No. 20

More Than a Puzzle

If I'm a puzzle,

you don't complete me.

You give me a life

to be much more

than a piece of wood.

No. 21

In Every Darkness

In the darkest of night,

you're the woodfire.

In the deepest ocean,

you're the lifeboat.

In the scariest place,

you're the hand of god.

No. 22

Eclipse

Oh how dumb was I,

in hunger of seeing her light,

I completely ignored her darkness.

For her beauty was like the moon,

I never understood the change

until she turned into

the darkness of the eclipse.

No. 23

I Love You

I love you so fucking much.

I don't know how to show it over text.

Here, I can only write it.

I really, really love you.

No. 24

A Life Together

I see my future with you.

You're with me in my car.

You're the one making me food

while I annoy you.

You're the one who sings with my guitar.

You're not just a person.

You're literally a part of me.

No. 25

You

I want only you to see me when I cry,

and the only reason I laugh

is you.

No. 26

Every Second

I want to spend every second with you.

Every last second of my life

will be with you.

No. 27

I Will Stay

I want to fight with you

and then realise

I can't live without you.

I want to hold you forever.

No. 28

Good and Bad

I will be with you

in both good and bad times.

To smile in good times,

and to wipe your tears

in bad times.

No. 29

Enough

I would rather starve with you

than eat the finest delicacy alone.

No. 30

Fly With Me

Instead of falling for me,

fly for me, my dear.

No. 31

Poison

The poison was your eyes.

The poison was your curses

wrapped in love.

The poison were your lips

next to mine

when I was at my lowest.

No. 32

Till Death

I love you till death,

and you are the reason

why I'm alive.

No. 33

Beacon

Even if the sun sets eternally

and darkness swallows the world,

I'll still find you

and stay with you

as the beacon of hope.

No. 34

That Laugh

When you're laughing uncontrollably

with your eyes closed,

all that matters

is that you can make me laugh like that.

No. 35

One Moment

Give me this moment of yours,

and my whole eternity

will be dedicated to you.

No. 36

Where We Were

I will stay

where we met,

where we laughed,

where we cried,

where we hugged,

where we kissed.

No. 37

Every Lifetime

"I would choose you

in every lifetime

even if it meant

losing you every time."
